Privacy Policy

Last updated: Jnuary 10, 2026

Smart Expense Tracker (“the App”) is developed by Vivek. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information collected from users.

Information We Collect

  • Expense data you enter: amounts, categories, descriptions, attachments, and any other transaction details you add remain on your device unless you export or share them manually.
  • Optional attachments: if you attach receipts or images, they are stored locally on your device.
  • Crash or performance data: Google Play may collect anonymized diagnostics as part of app distribution. We do not receive personally identifiable crash data.

How We Use Your Information

  • Display and organize your expenses, income, and reports.
  • Generate PDF exports when you request them and share via the Android share sheet.
  • Improve stability and performance based on aggregated diagnostics from Google Play (if available).

Data Storage and Sharing

All expense and attachment data stays on your device. The App does not upload your personal data to our servers. When you export or share a PDF, the file is generated locally and only shared with the destinations you choose (email, cloud storage, etc.). We do not sell or disclose your data to third parties.

Permissions

  • Storage access: required to save PDF reports and attachments you choose to export.

Security

Because data remains on your device, please keep your device secure with a PIN or biometric lock. We recommend regular backups if you install the app on multiple devices.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us to remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
